Pancakes

Recipe Overview: A pancake recipe
Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • Dry Ingredients
    • 2 cups white whole wheat flour or all-purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    • 3 tablespoons granulated sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Wet Ingredients
    • 2 1/4 cups buttermilk, yogurt, or milk
    • 2 large eggs, separated
    • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • For Serving
    • Butter for the pan
    • Butter for serving
    • Syrup or fruit if desired

Steps

  1. Sift flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt together in a large bowl.
  2. Make a well in the center and add milk, melted butter, and egg; mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.
  6. Pour the batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form and the bottoms are golden.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
