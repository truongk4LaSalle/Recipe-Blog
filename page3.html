Waffles

Recipe Overview: A waffle recipe
Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 10 minutes Servings: 10 waffles

Ingredients

  • Dry Ingredients
    • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 tablespoon baking powder
    • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • Wet Ingredients
    • 2 cups milk
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
    • 2 large eggs
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • For Serving
    • Maple syrup
    • Butter
    • Fruit or powdered sugar if desired

Steps

  1. Preheat your waffle iron.
  2. Whisk the dry ingredients together.
  3. To a medium bowl, add the milk, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour in the melted butter and whisk the wet ingredients together.
  5. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir just until combined.
  6. Grease the hot waffle iron with butter, vegetable oil, or cooking spray.
  7. Cook until golden brown.
  8. Place the waffles on a wire rack until ready to serve.
